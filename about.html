About Our Superhero Hub

Welcome to our Superhero Hub, a tribute to the iconic Avengers! This website is a project built to showcase our favorite heroes, their incredible stories, and their unique powers.

Our Mission

We aim to celebrate the legacy of superheroes by providing detailed bios and power stats, inspiring fans and aspiring coders alike. This site is crafted with HTML and CSS as part of a learning journey in web development.

Why the Avengers?

The Avengers represent teamwork, courage, and innovation—qualities we admire and aim to reflect in our coding projects. Explore each hero’s page to learn more about their origins and abilities!
